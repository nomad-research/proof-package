# Nomad v6 Observation Framework - Public Proof Pack

This repository contains a clean, agent-friendly public proof pack consisting of 5 historical detection sessions generated by the Nomad v6 observation framework. These sessions have been structured into standardized, machine-readable JSON files, allowing agents and developers to easily backtest the framework's calibration and edge against historical market data.

The Nomad testing methodology operates by detecting fundamental expectation violations in real-time, mapping the transmission of that information through market participant classes, and predicting the cascade of secondary and tertiary effects. It identifies the remaining gradient energy before full market pricing occurs and establishes strict, machine-executable conditions (ACKs) to validate or invalidate the thesis. The system then evaluates its own performance against the actual market outcomes to continuously calibrate its predictive accuracy.

Calibration scores measure the framework's accuracy in predicting the exact sequence, timing, and market impact of the identified cascades. A calibration score of 8.5 or higher indicates a valid hit, meaning the framework successfully anticipated the market's response and generated actionable alpha.
